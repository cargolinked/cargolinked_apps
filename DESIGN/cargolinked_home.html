Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about becoming a CargoLinked agent

How do I become a CargoLinked agent?

Becoming a CargoLinked agent is simple. Create your account, complete your profile with business credentials, upload required documentation (insurance, licenses), and once verified, you can start browsing and quoting on freight requests immediately.

What are the fees for using CargoLinked?

CargoLinked charges a small service fee only when you successfully complete a job. There are no upfront costs, monthly fees, or charges for submitting quotes. Our success is tied to your success.

How quickly do I get paid after completing a job?

Payments are processed within 24-48 hours after successful delivery confirmation. We offer multiple payment methods including direct deposit, PayPal, and check payments to ensure you receive your earnings quickly and securely.

What insurance requirements do I need?

All agents must maintain current commercial auto insurance, general liability insurance, and cargo insurance. Minimum coverage amounts vary by cargo type and value. Our team can help connect you with insurance providers if needed.

Can I work with multiple freight platforms?

Yes, there are no exclusivity requirements. Many of our successful agents use CargoLinked alongside other platforms. However, we encourage focusing on quality service to build your reputation and earn repeat business from premium clients.
